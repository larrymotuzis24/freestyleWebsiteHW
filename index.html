About

Enough of the WHAT IF debates!! We are building a program in which two teams will be created by a user, the user will get to decide what player past or present they want on the each of teams. We will determine a winner between the two teams using the data that we have determined contributes the most to winning. Create your two teams from the player pool below and see who would win the game!

Data Used

  • PPG
  • Defensive Rating
  • Players Plus/Minus
  • Players Carrer Win %
  • Field Goal Percentage
